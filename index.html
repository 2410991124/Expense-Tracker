Four rules for less money stress

Your money, Your future, Your choice

Age Your Money

If you could use last month's money to pay current month's bills, wouldn't it feels great. You'd always be one step ahead. It feels so great.

Embrace Your True Expenses

Some expenses like car repairs & holiday vacation,etc. are non-monthly expenses. So, break these expenses into smaller monthly expenses. means -Save some part of money every month for these unexpected & non-monthly expense.

Categorize Your Expenses

To manage expenses , divide your expenses into categories like transport, vacation, rent, party, Electricity, food, etc. This will help you identify where most of your money is going & need cut down and adjustment.

Roll With The Punches

Be Flexible! as there's no such thing as a normal month. So, stop taking financial stress. When life changes, change your spending plan with it. No guilt required - it's your money.
