Frequently Asked Questions

What is Track it?

Track it is an all-in-one expense management platform. You can track every receipt, manage expenses, all with a couple of clicks.

Who is Track it for?

Track it is the perfect expense management tool for everyone:

  • Housewife: Track household expenses like groceries, cleaning, etc.
  • Employers: Manage budgets, house expenses, office expenses, etc.
  • Accounting: View expense requests on one dashboard, reimburse instantly, and auto-import entries into your preferred accounting software.

How much does it cost?

Track it is completely free to use as an individual. You just need to open the website and log in to your account.

How do I get Started?

Getting started with Track it is easy! Just sign up with your email or phone number, and you can start uploading receipts and tracking expenses right away.

How quickly can I get set up?

Track it is ready to use in seconds! Just sign up with your email or phone number to open your account, and you can now use it right away!

What kind of expenses can I track?

With Track it, you can track every kind of expenses like homely expense, office expenses, clubbing, travelling, etc. and also savings.

Can I use Track it if my company doesn't use it?

Yes! Employees and contractors can use Expensify to track and manage their expenses even if their company doesn't use Track it. You can submit your expenses to your employer electronically.

What does Track it integrate with?

Track it integrates with notifications, adding transactions, showing transactions, savings and keeping track out of it everything.
